# Neon Fighters '98 - 24-Bit Edition

Welcome to the upgraded 24-Bit Edition of Neon Fighters '98! Featuring 10 unique characters, enhanced parallax graphics, and local 2-player support.

## Starting the Game
1. Wait for the "INSERT COIN" screen to appear.
2. Click the **"PRESS START"** button with your mouse to begin the match and initialize the audio.
3. **Mode Selection:** Choose between "1 PLAYER (VS CPU)" or "2 PLAYERS (LOCAL VS)".
4. **Character Selection:** 
   * **Player 1:** Use `Arrow Keys` to browse and `A` or `Space` to select.
   * **Player 2:** Use `I, J, K, L` to browse and `Z` or `Enter` to select.

## Controls (Keyboard & Mouse)
Make sure you click on the game window so it has keyboard focus.

### Player 1
* **Movement:** `Arrow Keys` (Up to Jump, Down to Crouch, Left/Right to Move)
* **Combat (Keyboard):**
  * `A`: Light Attack
  * `S`: Heavy Attack
  * `D`: Kick
  * `Space`: Special Attack
  * `Shift` (Left or Right): Block
* **Combat (Mouse):**
  * `Left Click`: Light Attack
  * `Right Click`: Heavy Attack
  * `Middle Click (Scroll Press)`: Special Attack

### Player 2
* **Movement:** `I` (Jump), `J` (Left), `K` (Crouch), `L` (Right)
* **Combat:**
  * `Z`: Light Attack
  * `X`: Heavy Attack
  * `C`: Kick
  * `V`: Special Attack
  * `B`: Block

## In-Game UI
* **PAUSE:** Click the "PAUSE" button in the bottom right corner of the game screen to pause the match.
* **RETURN:** Click the "RETURN" button to immediately end the match and return to the main menu.

## Music Player
At the bottom of the arcade cabinet, you will find the music control panel:
* **PLAY/PAUSE:** Toggles the retro synthwave background music.
* **NEXT:** Skips to the next music track.

## Rules
* Deplete the opponent's health bar to win a round.
* The first fighter to win **2 rounds** wins the match!
* If the timer reaches 0, the fighter with the most health wins the round.
* **Note:** In VS CPU mode, the CPU will wait until the timer reaches 90 seconds before it begins attacking.
